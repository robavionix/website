Trust is one of the strongest drivers of e-commerce performance. Customers need to understand the product, believe the store is reliable, and feel safe before they purchase.

Design can support that trust with clear product information, consistent pricing, real reviews, simple return policies, and visible support options.

> Trust grows when a store removes uncertainty at the exact moment a customer is deciding.

Mobile experience is especially important. Buttons, product options, shipping details, and payment summaries must be easy to read and use on smaller screens.

## Make Confidence Visible

Place delivery, returns, guarantees, and support details near product and checkout decisions.

When customers do not need to search for reassurance, they are more likely to finish the purchase.
