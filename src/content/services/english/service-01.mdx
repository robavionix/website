Modern companies collect data across websites, apps, operations, and customer touchpoints. We help teams understand where that data lives, how it moves, and which controls are needed to protect it.

Our data tracking security service combines visibility, access review, risk monitoring, and practical documentation so teams can work confidently with sensitive information.

## Security Services

We review analytics tools, forms, integrations, data storage, and user permissions to identify the places where information can be exposed, duplicated, or poorly governed.
